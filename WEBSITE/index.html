Our Story

Three Decades of Precision.

Established in 1996 in the heart of Bhopal, Joshi's Printers began with a simple mission: to provide the local community with uncompromising print quality.

Under the leadership of Nitin Joshi, we have evolved from a traditional press into a high-tech printing powerhouse. Whether it is a single pack of premium business cards or a massive export order of custom packaging boxes, we treat every print with the same mathematical precision.

We don't just put ink on paper — we bring your brand's vision to life.

Production typically takes 3–7 working days depending on order size and complexity. Once dispatched, delivery across India takes 2–5 business days via our trusted logistics partners. We always share a tracking link once your order ships.
Yes! Our in-house design team can create artwork for your order at a nominal charge. Just share your logo, brand colours, and any reference images and we'll take care of the rest. We provide a PDF proof for your approval before printing begins.
We accept PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), CDR (CorelDRAW), and high-resolution PSD or PNG files (minimum 300 DPI). All files should be in CMYK colour mode. If you're unsure, just send us what you have and we'll guide you.
